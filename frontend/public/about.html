Our Mission

RAKHTSEVAK is a revolutionary platform connecting blood donors with those in need. Our mission is to create a seamless, efficient, and reliable blood donation ecosystem that saves lives across communities.

Key Features

User-Friendly Registration

Simple and intuitive registration process for both donors and recipients with minimal steps to get started quickly.

Dual Interface System

"Become a Donor": Register as a blood donor
"Find a Donor": Search for available donors with detailed profiles

Comprehensive Donor Profiles

View donor information including name, blood group, location, and contact details for easy coordination.

Donation Cooldown System

Smart tracking that prevents donors from donating more frequently than every 3 months, with visual indicators showing availability status.

Availability Updates

Donors can easily update their availability status to ensure the platform always has current information.

Advanced Search

Find donors by specific blood group and location to quickly connect with the most relevant donors.

Real-Time Notifications

Instant alerts to users about blood requirements and donation opportunities in their area.

Plasma Donation

Dedicated section for plasma donation, expanding the life-saving capabilities of our platform.

Donor Recognition

Badge system that rewards and recognizes donors based on their donation count, encouraging continued participation.
